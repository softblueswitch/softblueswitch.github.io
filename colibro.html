Bilingual reader learning application for kids

Colibro is an educator bilingual reader app for children to learn a second language. The platform backend allows for the parent or teacher to build courses consisting of classes, assignments and tests for the student. As the UX and UI designer, I contributed to the evolution of the reader application IA through user stories; functional requirements; wireframing and prototyping the design itteratons. This was pivotal to craft an engaging and educational experience. I also conducted user research, to ensure an intuitive interface. Design guides were produced for various screen sizes, ensuring consistency, while usability testing with children refined the app's effectiveness. The result was a successful and enjoyable app, helping young learners develop language skills alongside their parent or teacher.
