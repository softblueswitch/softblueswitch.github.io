Help-seaking website for blokes who don't

Mental health organisation Beyond Blue wanted to build a website to target men who need to seek help but cannot start the process. Working for Marmalade in Melbourne as the lead UX designer I engaged with the client to understand the issue and generate very generalised personas of this user group. We conducted research and demographic analysis to build user stories for a sensitive issue, using humour and low-prompting functions to establish trust in the user. I built out the high level AI structure then wireframes and prototypes for internal testing.
