Learning platform content library

Pace is an online learning platform from Pearson Education, a global educational publishing and assessment company. Pace supports students and educators in Australian secondary schools and provides a range of digital resources, tools and content aligned with Australian educational curricula for integtartion into MS Teams and Google Classroom.
I was the UX lead on the development of the MVP, I managed the design system and the generation of digital assets, while maintaining alignment with user requirements, integration of social platforms and stakeholder business objectives.
