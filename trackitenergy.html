SAAS Product for Petrochemical and Mining industries

Trackit Energy is a Software as a Service platform that was designed and developed for Petrochemical and Mining industries. While working at Efinity I worked on the UX revisions of the modular functionality, as the product was run through testing with Petronas in Malaysia.
Key to the design strategy, the modular, in-browser accessibility of the product was pivotal in the industry. There were already many clumsy bespoke data-management Google Docs that were considered industry-standard, Look Ahead being one of them. Trackit is now used by Shell, Chevron, OMV, BHP and Eni as a scalable service product that enables contractors to self-manage schedules, regulatory sign-offs and handovers, while having access to operational oversight when onboarding.
