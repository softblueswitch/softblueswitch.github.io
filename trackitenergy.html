SAAS Product for Petrochemical and Mining industries

Trackit Energy is a Software as a Service platform that was designed and developed for Petrochemical and Mining industries. It is a modular based platform that was primarily designed for helicopter scheduling and people on board management. You choose the modules you want and the time frame that you need the platform for and we'll have you up and running within 24 hours. Whether you need one instance or many for your global organisation we can scale rapidly.






FailChips

FailChips is a play on the mispronunciation of "MailChimp", for whom this site was produced. The idea was that these chips are "fails" because they are already crumbled to tiny fragments, the crumbs that you find in the bottom of your bag of chips, where all the flavour and salt has concentrated. These chips consisted of just those crumbs and it was possible to buy actual bags of them in stores across the US. I worked as the interactive producer for this site, fielding clients decisions through our partner agency, Droga 5 in New York.
