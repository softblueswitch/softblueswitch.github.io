3D Mobile Web GL Game

The 100K Prize Dive was a three-month, mobile-only campaign that utilised Web GL and current mobile CPU speeds to generate a fast-moving browser-based game. I was the digital producer at creative agency Colenso and worked with 3rd parties including 3D modellers, production partners, procurement specialists and hosting agencies.
