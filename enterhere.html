A single hub for your healing practice

Enter Here is a platform for individuals seeking a professional therapist with whom they can connect and trust. In my hybrid role as UX Strategist and Product Owner, I played an instrumental part in identifying user journeys of both therapists and help-seekers. As the platform had it's foundation in the social mechanic between users and different user groups, we had to keep the focus pulled on the immersive experience and sharing functionality that was to drive uptake and continual engagement, while maintaining the professionalism that the profiles and behaviour of the therapist users needed to exhibit. I worked closely with the UI design and development teams to incorporate innovative solutions, prioritising the delivery of a comforting haven for those seeking help. My primary objective was to create an impactful platform that is as rewarding for the therapists as it is comforting and accessible for the seeker users.
