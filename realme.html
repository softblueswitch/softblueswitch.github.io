Government ID application for mobile

RealMe is a mobile application initiative from the New Zealand Department of Internal Affairs as an easy way for people to prove who they are without having to navigate between channels. The agency was tasked with developing an MVP with specific functionality for university students. As the UX designer on this project I developed prototypes to serve as a proof of concept, with key functionalities to verify and install confidence in key stakeholders, of the value and benefits that the RealMe platform can deliver. The wireframes and prototypes demonstrated what the future state could look like with an integrated biometric login that is able to verify who you are, as well as establishing a more MVP state, where you self-assert. Both were used as testing mechanisms to explore and validate the feasibility and opportunity of the concept
